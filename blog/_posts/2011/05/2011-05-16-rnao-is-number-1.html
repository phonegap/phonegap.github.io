Hey Everyone,

Last week, RNAO was the top paid medical iPhone app in iTunes Canada. RNAO stands for the Registered Nurses' Association of Ontario. The app was made using PhoneGap by Nitobi. It delivers professional development programs and also publishes the "Nursing Best Practice Guidelines". If you want to download the app on either iTunes/Android/BB check out http://www.phonegap.com/app/rnao-nursing-best-practice-guideli/.

Also, check out the case study about RNAO on the Nitobi website at http://nitobi.com/casestudies/rnao/ Cheers, -Steve
