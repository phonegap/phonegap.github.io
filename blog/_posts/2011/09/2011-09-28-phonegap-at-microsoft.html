Come join our PhoneGap meetup at the Microsoft campus in Redmond, WA. on* October 7 from 7-9pm*. It's totally free!

This informal, fun meetup will feature an introduction to PhoneGap. See a hands-on tutorial showing you how to use your HTML/JavaScript skills to build applications for mobile platforms including iPhone, Google Android, Blackberry, Symbian and Palm.

In the second half, you'll see a demo of PhoneGap for Windows Phone 7. Nitobi worked closely with Microsoft to bring PhoneGap to WP7 devices and we opened up the beta in early September. Jesse MacFadyen, Nitobi's Senior Developer, will give a rundown on how to get started and how it works.

If that wasn't enough, we'll be throwing in free pizza, beer and even some XBox 360 giveaways.

Sign up for the event.

Directions

The Meetup will take place on the Microsoft Campus in Redmond, WA:

3720 159th Ave NE Building 34 Quinault conference room
